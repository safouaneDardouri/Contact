A New Gaming Era

Dear Gamers and Supporters, Hello and welcome to you all. I’m Hu-min, founder of Wonder People. Back in 2001 I set on this journey with a studio named ‘Neople’, and with our flagship game: ‘Dungeon and Fighter’, we became the most successful gaming company in South Korea.

Achieving 1st place was an amazing experience, but having no plans forward I soon lost direction. I ended up selling my company and left for America where I fulfilled my long dream of studying in Berkelee. With that done I then moved back to Korea where my new phase of life was filled with investing in various start-ups and venture companies like WeMakePrice.

During this time I had an enlightenment: ‘Perhaps we’re entering an era where everyone has a gaming device of their own.’ I recognized that my most favorite company Nintendo had sold 100 million consoles at the time, and this awoke me that with the vast distribution of mobile phones and electronic devices, soon 5 billion people in the world could be holding a gaming device of their own. Truly exciting times.

We’re connected to our phone’s day in and day out; this translated to me that everyone has reached the stage where we’re each holding a gaming device 24 hours a day. It’s very challenging or impossible for someone to carry a Nintendo or PlayStation with them all the time, but with smartphones integrated so deeply in our lives we are now standing in the doorways of an explosion of gaming market where the next Big Bang is happening. The thought simply shocked me.

I’ve heard my share of investment pitches in gaming companies, but since my success in Neople I always had told myself that my time in the industry was over and that I’d never run another gaming studio again. I didn’t invest in other gaming companies rather than a few acquainted one’s for old time’s sake.

But a new gaming era and its’ horizon changed everything for me. It was time for me to take the leap of faith once again.

New Goals and New Challenges

“1 Billion DAU games in 10 Years”

This was my new goal: making the most beloved gaming company in the world’s history.
I have concrete belief that if I can organize a company where the best gamers would like to join in, we’ll eventually have the best squad resulting in a magnificent game. It will take time, but this was also how Neople and Dungeon and Fighters were conceived. We’re not on the top gaming company on your application list just yet, but we are certainly headed that way and my word stays true in achieving that goal.

Looking back to my own history, I was fortunate to achieve a few things: Graduating Seoul National University as the President of the Student Council, Berklee, WeMakePrice and Professional Baseball Player. All of which people had told me that it wouldn’t be possible but I alone had self-conviction. I never stopped believing and announcing that my goals will come true, and though some goals took time to harvest, all of them came true. I had a failure streak of 18 games until finally Dungeon and Fighters was born through tenacity and unwithered passion.
If you can relate to my life and goals. We will make it ours. If you heart beats at the thought of games, join Wonder People and the Wonderful brigade on a course to destiny as the OnePiece did.

“1 Billion DAU. Are you with us?”

CEO Hu-Min
